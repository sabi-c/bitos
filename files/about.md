# About BITOS

BITOS (Built-In Thinking Operating System) is an open-source AI companion device.

Hardware: Raspberry Pi Zero 2W, 240x280 OLED display, single button input, speaker, microphone.

Software: Python, FastAPI, Anthropic Claude, local SQLite storage.

Designed for focused interaction without the distractions of a phone or laptop. One button, one screen, one conversation at a time.
