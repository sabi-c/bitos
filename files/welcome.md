# Welcome to BITOS

BITOS is your personal AI companion device. It lives on your desk, in your pocket, or wherever you need a thinking partner.

The device runs a local AI agent that knows your tasks, messages, and calendar. It speaks when spoken to and stays quiet when you need focus.

Navigate with a single button: tap to scroll, double-tap to select, hold to go back. Triple-tap for page navigation.

Everything stays private. Your data lives on the device and your own server. No cloud required.
