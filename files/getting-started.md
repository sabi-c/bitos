# Getting Started

## Power On
Hold the side button for 2 seconds. The boot animation plays while the system initializes.

## Connect Wi-Fi
If no network is configured, a QR code appears. Scan it with your phone to open the companion app and enter Wi-Fi credentials.

## First Conversation
Tap the button once to start recording. Speak your message, then tap again to send. The AI processes your voice and responds on screen and through the speaker.

## Navigation
- Single tap: scroll or next item
- Double tap: select or confirm
- Triple tap: previous item or next page
- Long press: go back

## Daily Use
The device shows a morning brief with your tasks, unread messages, and calendar. Ask it anything throughout the day. It remembers context from previous conversations.

## Settings
Hold the button from the home screen to access settings. Adjust font size, text speed, AI model, and integrations.
